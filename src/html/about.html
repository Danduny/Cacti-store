Who are we?

We are the leading cactus store within the European Union. We have amassed over 500,000 sales, within the year of 2019.


It creeps! It crawls! It clones itself! The creeping devil is truly one of the cactus family’s most shudder-inducing members. Instead of standing erect, as do most long-stemmed cacti, the creeping devil lies on the ground and creeps along like something out of a ’50s horror flick.
